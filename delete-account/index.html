Delete Your WiseBlood Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the app.

Steps to delete your account:

  • Open the WiseBlood app
  • Tap your profile picture (top left) to open the menu
  • Scroll down and tap "Delete My Data"
  • Confirm deletion in the dialog

What gets deleted:

  • Your account and profile information
  • All family profiles
  • All uploaded blood reports and analysis results
  • Health scores and trend data
  • Medicine reminders

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
