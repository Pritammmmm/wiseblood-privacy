Introduction

Welcome to WiseBlood. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the WiseBlood mobile application and related services.

By using WiseBlood, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of the application immediately.

WiseBlood is designed to help you understand your blood test reports through AI-powered analysis. We take the responsibility of handling your health data seriously and use industry-standard security measures to protect it.

Information We Collect

1. Account Information

When you create an account with WiseBlood, we collect:

  • Full name provided during profile setup
  • Email address associated with your Google account
  • Date of birth for age-related health reference ranges
  • Gender for gender-specific health parameter analysis
  • Profile photo if provided through your Google account

2. Health & Medical Data

To provide our core analysis service, we collect and process:

  • Blood test reports uploaded in PDF, JPG, or PNG format
  • Extracted blood test parameters including hemoglobin, glucose, HDL, LDL, total cholesterol, triglycerides, thyroid markers, liver markers, kidney markers, CBC values, vitamin levels, and other standard blood parameters
  • Health scores and analysis results generated from reports
  • Historical trend data compiled from multiple reports
  • AI-generated insights, advice, and parameter summaries

3. Family Profile Data

If you use family profiles available with Plus and Family plans, we additionally collect:

  • Full name of each family member
  • Date of birth of each family member
  • Gender of each family member
  • Relationship to the primary account holder, such as spouse, parent, or child
  • Individual reports and health data for each family member, stored separately under their profile

4. Subscription & Payment Data

When you subscribe to a paid plan, the following is processed:

  • Subscription plan type such as Plus Monthly, Plus Annual, Family Monthly, or Family Annual
  • Subscription status including active, expired, or cancelled
  • Purchase dates and renewal dates
  • Transaction identifiers for purchase verification

Important: All payment processing is handled by Google Play and RevenueCat. We do not collect, store, or access your credit card numbers, bank account details, or other direct financial information.

5. Device & Technical Data

To ensure the app functions correctly and troubleshoot issues:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • App version number
  • General error logs and crash reports
  • Network connectivity status

How We Use Your Information

Core Service Delivery

  • Analyze uploaded blood test reports to extract parameters and provide health insights
  • Generate personalized health scores based on test results and standard medical reference ranges
  • Display historical blood parameter trends across multiple reports
  • Provide AI-generated health advice and recommendations based on report findings
  • Manage and display separate profiles for family members

Account & Service Management

  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Process your subscription plan and entitlements
  • Enforce fair usage limits to maintain service quality
  • Respond to support inquiries

Service Improvement

  • Identify and fix bugs, errors, and technical issues
  • Improve user experience and app performance
  • Develop new features using aggregated, anonymized usage data

Third-Party Services

WiseBlood integrates with trusted third-party services. Each service has its own privacy policy governing its handling of data.

Google Firebase

Used for secure authentication via Google Sign-In. Firebase manages login sessions, authentication tokens, and account security. It does not access your health data or blood reports.

RevenueCat

Used for subscription and in-app purchase management. RevenueCat processes subscription status and purchase verification using a unique user identifier, but does not have access to your health data or reports.

Cloudinary

Used for secure cloud storage of uploaded report files. Files are stored with protected URLs and are accessible only through authenticated requests tied to your account.

AI Analysis Service

Used to extract text and parameters from uploaded blood test reports. Report data is sent only for processing. No personal identifiers are sent beyond the report content needed for analysis.

Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your personal and health information seriously. The following safeguards are in place:

Encryption

  • All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Sensitive data stored on servers is encrypted at rest using industry-standard methods
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on device using platform-backed secure storage

Authentication & Access Control

  • JWT-based authentication protects account access
  • Access tokens expire after 15 minutes
  • Refresh tokens expire after 7 days
  • Every API request is authenticated and authorized to limit users to their own data

Infrastructure

  • Servers are hosted on secure cloud infrastructure with regular updates and monitoring
  • Database access is restricted to authorized services and protected by network-level rules
  • Regular security reviews are conducted to identify and address vulnerabilities

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We value your privacy and are committed to keeping your data confidential.

We Do Not

  • Sell your personal or health data to third parties
  • Share blood test results or health scores with advertisers, brokers, or marketing companies
  • Use your health data for targeted advertising
  • Provide your data to insurers, employers, or similar entities
  • Allow third-party services to access health data beyond what is strictly necessary for app functionality

Limited Sharing

  • With listed third-party providers only to operate the app, including authentication, payments, storage, and report analysis
  • When required by law in response to a valid court order, subpoena, or government request
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of WiseBlood, our users, or the public as permitted by law

Data Retention

  • Account data and health information are retained while your account remains active
  • Deleted reports are permanently removed from servers and cloud storage within 30 days
  • Deleting a family member profile permanently deletes all associated profile data
  • Deleting your account permanently removes all data from servers within 30 days
  • Anonymized, aggregated statistical data may be retained for service improvement
  • Encrypted backup copies may persist for up to 90 days before automatic purge

Your Rights & Controls

Data Access & Portability

  • View stored reports, scores, and trends inside the app
  • Request a complete copy of your data by emailing support@wiseblood.app

Data Deletion

  • Delete individual blood test reports from History
  • Delete family member profiles and their associated data from Family Profiles
  • Delete your entire account and all associated data from Settings

Subscription Management

  • Cancel your subscription through Google Play settings
  • Change your plan at any time while retaining existing data

Communication

  • Contact support@wiseblood.app for privacy-related questions, concerns, or requests
  • Request clarification on how your data is processed

Children's Privacy

WiseBlood is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

The family profiles feature allows parents and guardians to manage health data for their children. In such cases, the parent or guardian is responsible for managing the child's data and must have the legal authority to consent on behalf of the child.

If we become aware that we collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take immediate steps to delete that information.

Medical Disclaimer

WiseBlood provides health information for educational and informational purposes only. The analysis, health scores, insights, and advice in the app are generated by artificial intelligence and are not intended to:

  • Replace professional medical advice or consultation
  • Serve as a medical diagnosis of any condition
  • Be used as a basis for starting, stopping, or modifying any medication or treatment
  • Substitute for routine check-ups and consultations with qualified healthcare providers

Always consult your doctor or a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, contact your local emergency services immediately.

WiseBlood, its developers, and affiliates are not liable for health decisions made based on app content. AI-generated analysis may vary in accuracy and should be verified by a qualified medical professional.

Cookies & Tracking

WiseBlood is a mobile application and does not use browser cookies. We do not engage in cross-app tracking or behavioral advertising.

We do not use analytics SDKs that track your behavior across other applications. The data we collect is directly related to your use of WiseBlood as described in this policy.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed on servers located in different countries. By using WiseBlood, you consent to the transfer of your information to facilities outside your country of residence where data protection laws may differ.

We ensure that international transfers comply with applicable data protection regulations and that appropriate safeguards are in place.
